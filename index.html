GAME CHARACTERS:
Who Takes The Cake?

Millions of games have been released since the beginning of time. Whether it's from retro games, arcade games, fps games, racing games, etc, we all have a few game characters we love more than pizza.This page highlights my top 4 game characters.

My Favourite Game Characters

Fuse is a character from a fps game called Apex Legends. Apex legends is a battle royale game that has a high skill ceiling and the journey to the top is fun (sometimes frustrating, but mostly fun).His abilities allow him to actively engage and attack other players instead of lying low and playing defensive (This depends on player style - I personally enjoy taking an active role on my team).

Abilities:

  • Passive: Carry Extra Grenades. Send grenades further & faster
  • Tactical: The Knuckle Cluster sends off explosive airburts
  • Ultimate: The Motherload sends a ring of fire that surrounds enemies

Collosus is a character from the game X-men. Through out the years, there have been several releases of the X-Men game, so I'm sure everyone here is familiar with Colossus. I personally played him when I was still rocking the Playstation 2. Shootig at, or attacking Collosus won't do much damage with his ability. Run!


Abilities:

  • Ultimate: Turn his body into an 'organic-steel' type state. protects him from most bullets, knives and other harmful attack objects.

Back again with another PS2 game I really enjoyed called Ratchet & Clank. This game was a feel good action-adventure game that was just fun to play. To be honest, sometimes I didn't even know what was happening but one thing I do know is that is was fun! The game creaters recently rebooted the game with a newer version called Ratchet & Clank Rift Apart. All the gadgets and utilities will have you missing work for weeks so I suggest you be careful.

Abilities:

  • Utilizes the gadgets provided at each level

Finally, we have Luigi from Super Mario. Super Mario is a platform game that most people have played. I love this game because it's very simple yet intruguing and fun. As to why I like Luigi more than Mario, uhmm..... I really don't know, I just do. All playable characters play the exact same role and have no different in ability, but Luigi just does it for me.


Abilities:

  • None. Just make sure you eat the mushroom and flower.
